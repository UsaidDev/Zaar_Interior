28 Years of Excellence: From Har to Zaar Interior

Established in 1999, Zaar Interior has been a stalwart presence in the interior design industry for over 28 years. Our journey began with pioneering kitchen models in Kannur, marking the inception of our commitment to innovation and excellence. Over the years, we have evolved and expanded, culminating in the establishment of our first factory in 2004. With each milestone, including the transition from Har Interior to Zaar Interior, we have remained steadfast in our dedication to delivering unparalleled craftsmanship and design solutions.
